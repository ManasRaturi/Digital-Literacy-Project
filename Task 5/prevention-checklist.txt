Stay Safe Online - Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

General Safety Tips: 

✔ Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts and enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
✔ Avoid clicking suspicious links or downloading unknown attachments from emails or messages.
✔ Always keep your devices and applications up-to-date to avoid known security issues.
✔ Never share your personal information such as OTP, passwords, ID, etc., with anyone.
✔ Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi and avoid accessing your accounts on public Wi-Fi.
✔ Always verify the information before sharing it to avoid the spread of fake news and misinformation.

Financial / UPI Safety Tips: 

✔ Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank information because banks never ask for it.
✔ Always verify the UPI ID and other payment information before transferring money.
✔ Never accept unknown ‘collect requests’ from unknown people in Google Pay or PhonePe.
✔ Never scan unknown QR codes.

Reporting Cyber Crime: 

✔ Report any cyber fraud or suspicious activity immediately by visiting the National Cyber Crime Portal - cybercrime.gov.in.
✔ Call the Cyber Crime Helpline - 1930 in case of immediate assistance in Financial Frauds.
