Social Media Do’s and Don’ts (For College Students) - 

Do’s:

Think before posting and ensure that the information shared is respectful and appropriate.
Protect your privacy by making the required changes.
Ensure that the information shared is authentic.
Maintain a positive online presence.
Use strong passwords.

Dont's: 

Don’t share your personal information like address, phone number, and passwords.
Don’t engage in cyberbullying.
Don’t share information that can harm your online reputation.
Don’t click on suspicious messages.
Don’t share your location and other information.
